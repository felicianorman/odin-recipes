Pad Thai

Description

This is an easy, quick, and most importantly - yummy - recipe of Pad Thai!

You only need a few ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken
  • 2 Santa Maria pad thai sauce
  • A few tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 2 packets of rice noodles
  • 4-5 carrots
  • 1/2 cabbage
  • 1 leek
  • 1 lime
  • 2 eggs
  • 200 gram peanuts

Steps

  1. Roughly shred the carrots, and shop the leek
  2. Cut the chicken into small pieces
  3. Crush the peanuts
  4. Boil water for the noodles
  5. Fry the chicken in oil on high heat until its done
  6. Meanwhile add the noodles and boil them for 3 minutes
  7. Add the vegetables and stir on medium heat for 2 minutes
  8. Cut up the noodles and add them to the chicken and vegetable mix
  9. Crack the eggs into your mix
  10. Add the pad thai sauce and soy sauce
  11. Serve with lime and crushed peanuts, enjoy!
