Salmon with potatoes and cold dill sauce

Description

A delicious classic! Why make things complicated? Fried salmon with cooked potatoes and some delicious sauce is the perfect meal for tonight.

Here's what you'll need:

  • 4 salmon
  • 8-10 potatoes (2-3 per salmon, you decide)
  • Salt, pepper and lemon pepper
  • 1 dl dill (frozen works fine)
  • 3 dl sour cream
  • 4 tablespoons mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Mix the sour cream, mayonnaise and dill together in a bowl and put in the fridge
  2. Wash the potatoes and cook for 20-25 minutes
  3. Season the salmon with salt, pepper and lemon pepper on each side
  4. Cook in your airfryer on 180Â°C for 10 minutes
  5. Serve and enjoy with the cold sauce!
