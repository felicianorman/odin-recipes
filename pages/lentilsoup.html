Lentil Soup with Rice

Description

Do you want to cook something fast and cheap tonight?

Then lentil soup with rice is the perfect choice!

You will need:

  • 2 x 500g red lenses
  • 6 carrots
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 leek
  • 4 tablespoons tomato puree
  • 2 x 500g cans crushed tomatoes
  • 2 packets of coconut milk
  • 2 tablespoons garam masala
  • 6 dl water
  • Limejuice
  • Rice
  • Naan bread

Steps

  1. Chop up the carrot, garlic and leek
  2. Fry in oil around 2 minutes
  3. Add garam masala, tomato puree and the cans of crushed tomatoes
  4. Wash the red lenses and add with the water
  5. Let it cook up and boil on medium heat for 15-20 minutes
  6. Meanwhile boil the rice
  7. Add the coconut milk, and warm the naan breads
  8. Add salt and some limejuice. Enjoy with the naan bread!
